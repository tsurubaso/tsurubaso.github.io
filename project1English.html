Sanbagierbo

A web and desktop platform dedicated to literary creation, artistic collaboration, and the promotion of creative works.

Project Objectives

Sanbagierbo is both an online platform and a desktop tool with the following main objectives:

  • Support writers throughout their creative process
  • Promote works from authors and collaborating artists
  • Provide a space for sharing and distributing creations
