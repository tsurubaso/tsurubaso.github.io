Sanbagierbo

A web and desktop platform dedicated to literary creation, artistic collaboration, and the promotion of creative works.

Project Objectives

Sanbagierbo is both an online platform and a desktop tool with the following main objectives:

  • Support writers throughout their creative process, from the initial idea to the finished work
  • Promote their works as well as those of collaborating artists (musicians, illustrators, sound designers, etc.)
  • Provide a platform for sharing and distributing artistic creations
